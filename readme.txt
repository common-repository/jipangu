Jipangu is a community-driven map for discovering Japan. This plugin connects creators' websites to Jipangu, displaying their spots on a map.

== Description ==

Jipangu is a community-driven map that gathers all the articles and videos about Japan made by the community. This platform offers a unique way to explore Japan through diverse perspectives and experiences shared by its users. By collecting and showcasing user-generated content, Jipangu serves as an invaluable resource for anyone interested in discovering the rich culture, history, and beauty of Japan. Whether you are planning a trip or simply want to learn more about the country, Jipangu provides an immersive and informative experience.

This plugin allows creators to seamlessly connect their websites to Jipangu. By integrating with Jipangu, creators can retrieve their own spots and display them on their websites using an interactive map. This functionality enhances user engagement and provides a visually appealing way to share personal experiences and recommendations about Japan. The plugin simplifies the process of content integration, making it easy for creators to showcase their contributions to the Jipangu community.

Jipangu relies on Google Maps services to display location spots. By using this plugin, you agree to the terms and policies of Google Maps. For more information, please review [Google Maps Terms of Service](https://cloud.google.com/maps-platform/terms?hl=en) and [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

== Installation ==

1. Upload `jipangu` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
